Mustard

Mustard is an open source auction and marketplace platform, similar to eBay. It is developed and maintained by Hamjoint CIC for Hamjoint, a marketplace dedicated to amateur radio and electronics.

Features

  • User accounts
  • Classifieds adverts
  • Email notifications
  • Auctions
  • Purchasing system
  • User feedback
  • Paypal support
  • Stripe support
  • Messaging
  • Photo & video uploads
  • Helpdesk & dispute resolution
  • Full documentation
