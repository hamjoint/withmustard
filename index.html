Mustard

Mustard is an open source auction and marketplace platform, similar to eBay. It is developed and maintained by Hamjoint CIC for Hamjoint, a marketplace dedicated to amateur radio and electronics.

Features

  • User accounts
  • Feedback system
  • Card processing
  • Messaging
  • Photo uploading
  • Email notifications
